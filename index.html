✦ GOA, INDIA — DEC 2025

5 Days in Goa

📅 Dec 20 – 26
👥 2 Travellers
💰 ₹38,500 est.
🌤️ 28°C avg
Day 1 — Arrival & South Goa
Saturday, 20 December 2025
⌄
09:00 AM
Arrive at Goa Airport (GOI)
Flight from Lucknow. Pre-book cab to hotel — Ola/Uber available outside arrivals.
✈️ Travel
11:30 AM
Check-in: Treehouse Boutique Hotel, Colva
₹2,800/night. Beach-facing rooms, free breakfast included.
🏨 Hotel
01:00 PM
Lunch at Martin's Corner
Famous Goan seafood restaurant. Try prawn curry rice & fish thali. ~₹600 for two.
🍽️ Food
03:30 PM
Colva Beach & Sunset Walk
2 km of uncrowded white sand. Best beach photography spot. Golden hour starts 6:00 PM.
📸 Sightseeing
08:00 PM
Dinner at Zeebop by the Sea
Beachside shack, live music, fresh catches grilled to order. ~₹900 for two.
🍽️ Food
Day 2 — Heritage & Old Goa
Sunday, 21 December 2025
⌄
09:00 AM
Basilica of Bom Jesus
UNESCO World Heritage Site. Houses the mortal remains of St. Francis Xavier. Free entry.
🏛️ Heritage
11:00 AM
Se Cathedral & Old Goa Churches Trail
Largest church in Asia. Walk between 5 churches — takes about 90 minutes. Bring water.
🏛️ Heritage
01:30 PM
Lunch at Viva Panjim
Panjim's most charming restaurant, in a 200-year-old house. Authentic Goan home cooking.
🍽️ Food
03:30 PM
Fontainhas Latin Quarter Walk
Goa's Portuguese heritage village. Colourful tile facades, cafés, local art galleries.
📸 Sightseeing
Day 3 — North Goa Party Scene
Monday, 22 December 2025
⌄
10:00 AM
Calangute & Baga Beach
Most popular stretch. Parasailing (~₹800), jet-ski rides, beach shopping. Arrive early for spots.
🏖️ Beach
07:00 PM
Tito's Lane, Baga
Goa's most famous nightlife strip. Mix of shacks, clubs & live music. Cover ~₹500/person.
🎵 Nightlife
Day 4 — Dudhsagar Falls & Spice Farm
Tuesday, 23 December 2025
⌄
07:00 AM
Dudhsagar Waterfall Day Trip
3-hour jeep ride into Western Ghats. 310m-tall four-tiered waterfall. Pack rain gear. ~₹1,200/person.
🌿 Nature
03:00 PM
Savoi Spice Plantation Tour
Walking tour through vanilla, nutmeg, cardamom. Includes traditional Goan lunch. ~₹600/person.
🌿 Experience
Day 5 — Departure Day
Wednesday, 13 January 2026
⌄
09:00 AM
Last Morning at Palolem Beach
Crescent-shaped paradise, perfect for a quiet goodbye. Rent a kayak (₹300/hr).
🏖️ Beach
12:00 PM
Check-out & Head to Airport
Evening flight back. Grab Bebinca (Goan cake) as a souvenir from the airport bakery.

Travel Tips

  • · Rent a scooter for ₹300/day — best way to get around North Goa
  • · Avoid peak-hour beach vendors — walk 500m from main entry for better prices
  • · Book Dudhsagar day trip 2 days in advance — spots fill up fast in Dec
  • · Carry cash — many beach shacks don't accept UPI
  • · Best swimming beaches: Palolem, Agonda, Morjim
